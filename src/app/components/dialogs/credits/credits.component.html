Build-A-Bettel is an open-source creation by the Bettelion Food Truck based on the open-source character creator framework hackrew.

A huge thank you to each and every one of the fifty contributors who poured their time and talents into bringing Build-A-Bettel into existence. Their dedication make it all possible.

The four month long journey of creating this game has been an incredible one, fueled by passion and shared effort. We hope you enjoy playing it as much as we enjoyed making it!
